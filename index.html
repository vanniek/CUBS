“The Balloon Game”

For the purpose of minimizing external distractions throughout the task, you will be required to keep your earphones on at all times

The Task:

You will be presented with a balloon and offered the chance to earn money by following the instructions below:

  • Each click will cause the balloon to incrementally inflate and points to be added to the scoreboard at the bottom right of the screen
  • Each balloon has been randomly assigned an anonymous threshold value. This value will change in each round.
  • Points will be added to the scoreboard up until the balloon reaches its threshold value, at which point the balloon is over inflated and explodes
  • If the balloon explodes, the total on your scoreboard will be reset to zero and you will receive no points
  • The money you receive at the end of each round will be calculated as a fraction of the total points you earn.
  • Therefore, if your balloon explodes, the total money you earn for that round will be $0
  • Likewise, if you choose to cash-out prior to the balloon exploding, your total monetary payoff will be greater than $0
  • If you have any questions at this moment, please raise your hands and someone will come and assist you.
